OpenHSI Camera

This will take images that are:

  1. Cropped to the usable region (use the calibrate notebook to update this)
  2. Smile corrected (again, use the calibrate notebook to update this)

You can control the:

  1. Exposure time (in milliseconds)
  2. Gain
